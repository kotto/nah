Mining Excellence

Extracting Africa's Mineral Wealth

Horeb Mining specializes in the sustainable extraction and processing of non-ferrous metals and minerals, supporting Africa's industrial development.

Company Overview

Founded in 2007, Horeb Mining has established itself as a leading mining company in Africa, specializing in the extraction and processing of non-ferrous metal ores. With operations in 5 African countries, we employ cutting-edge technologies and sustainable practices to minimize environmental impact while maximizing resource utilization.

Our comprehensive mining portfolio includes exploration, extraction, processing, and refining operations across various mineral resources. We are committed to responsible mining practices that benefit local communities and contribute to Africa's industrial development.

Non-ferrous Metals

Extraction and processing of copper, zinc, aluminum, and other valuable non-ferrous metals.

Quarrying

Specialized extraction of industrial minerals and construction materials from quarries.

Mineral Refining

Advanced processing and refining of mineral oils and other resources.

Our Approach

At Horeb Mining, we believe in a sustainable approach to resource extraction that balances economic development with environmental stewardship and social responsibility. Our operations are guided by international best practices and rigorous standards to ensure the safety of our workers, protection of the environment, and positive impact on local communities.

"Mining is not just about extracting resources from the earth – it's about doing so in a way that respects the environment, empowers local communities, and contributes to sustainable development. At Horeb, we're committed to mining with purpose."

— Dr. Samuel Mwangi, CEO of Horeb Mining
