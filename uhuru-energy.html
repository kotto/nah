Renewable Energy

Powering Africa's Sustainable Future

Uhuru Energy is at the forefront of Africa's renewable energy revolution, delivering clean, reliable power solutions across the continent.

Company Overview

Founded in 2010, Uhuru Energy has rapidly established itself as a leading renewable energy provider in Africa. Our name "Uhuru," meaning "freedom" in Swahili, reflects our mission to liberate African communities from energy poverty through sustainable solutions.

With operations in 7 African countries and growing, we develop, finance, build, and operate utility-scale solar, wind, and hydroelectric power projects. Our integrated approach ensures reliable energy delivery while minimizing environmental impact.

Solar Energy

Harnessing Africa's abundant sunshine with cutting-edge photovoltaic technology.

Wind Power

Strategic wind farms in high-yield locations across coastal and highland regions.

Hydroelectric

Environmentally responsible hydro projects providing consistent base load power.

Our Approach

At Uhuru Energy, we believe in a holistic approach to energy development that considers environmental impact, community needs, and economic sustainability. Our projects are designed not just to generate power, but to create lasting positive change in the communities we serve.
