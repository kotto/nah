Premium Aviation Services

Connecting Africa with Excellence in the Skies

Skyline Aviation delivers world-class aviation services, connecting people, businesses, and opportunities across the African continent and beyond.

Company Overview

Established in 2008, Skyline Aviation has grown to become one of Africa's premier aviation companies. With a commitment to safety, reliability, and exceptional service, we've earned a reputation for excellence in the aviation industry.

Our comprehensive range of aviation services includes commercial passenger flights, cargo transportation, private jet charters, and specialized aviation solutions. With a modern fleet and highly trained professionals, we connect major African cities and provide vital links to international destinations.

Commercial Flights

Connecting major African cities with reliable, comfortable passenger service.

Cargo Services

Efficient transportation of goods with specialized handling capabilities.

Private Charter

Luxury private jet services for executives and VIP clients.

Our Commitment

At Skyline Aviation, safety is our highest priority. We maintain rigorous standards that exceed international requirements, ensuring the wellbeing of our passengers, crew, and cargo. Our commitment to operational excellence is matched by our dedication to customer service, creating a seamless travel experience from booking to arrival.

Our Fleet

Skyline Aviation operates a modern, fuel-efficient fleet maintained to the highest international standards.

Passenger Fleet

Our passenger fleet includes Boeing 737s, Airbus A320s, and Embraer E-Jets, offering comfort and reliability for short and medium-haul routes.

Capacity from 76 to 180 passengers

Cargo Fleet

Our dedicated cargo fleet includes Boeing 747F and 767F freighters, equipped with specialized handling systems for various cargo types.

Capacity up to 120 tons per aircraft

Executive Fleet

Our executive fleet features Gulfstream G650, Bombardier Global, and Cessna Citation jets for premium private charter services.

Luxurious comfort for 8-16 passengers
