Sion Aviation Industry provides comprehensive aviation services including maintenance, passenger transport, freight, and specialized support operations.

Company Overview

Established in 2024, Sion Aviation Industry has rapidly grown to become a specialized aviation services provider focused on aircraft maintenance, passenger transport, freight services, and transportation support activities. With a team of highly skilled aviation professionals, we deliver reliable and efficient services to clients across Africa and beyond.

Our comprehensive range of services is designed to meet the diverse needs of the aviation industry, from routine maintenance and repair to specialized passenger and cargo transport solutions. We pride ourselves on our technical expertise, commitment to safety, and dedication to customer satisfaction.

Maintenance & Repair

Comprehensive aircraft maintenance, repair, and overhaul services by certified technicians.

Passenger Transport

Scheduled and charter passenger services to domestic and international destinations.

Freight Services

Specialized cargo transport solutions for various industries and requirements.

Our Approach

At Sion Aviation, safety is our highest priority. We adhere to the strictest international aviation standards and regulations, ensuring the safety and security of our passengers, crew, and cargo. Our commitment to excellence extends to every aspect of our operations, from maintenance procedures to customer service.
